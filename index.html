    The ocean

    The ocean is where I feel free. The colour, the smell, and the sound make me feel zen. It is beautiful, and full of unknowns. You never know what you will discover - and to me, the ocean and my work are one in the same. There are so many aspects to it, and I never know which way I’ll be taken next. A blank canvas can be infinite things and I have the power to chose what to do with it. I’m continually discovering new medias and styles, and continually growing as a designer.
