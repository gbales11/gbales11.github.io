The ocean

The ocean is where I feel free. The colour, the smell, and the sound make me feel zen. It is beautiful, and full of unknowns. You never know what you will discover - and that is how I feel about Graphic Design. There are so many aspects to it, and I never know which way I’ll be taken next. A blank canvas, or page can be infinite things and I have the power to chose what to do with it - I am free within my work. I’m continually discovering new medias and styles, continually growing as a designer. Dive in with me, let’s explore the unknowns.
